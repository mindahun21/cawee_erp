PLAN STATUS MACHINE:
draft → submitted → approved | (rejected → draft)
approved → closed (auto: when hired >= vacancies_needed)
Editable when: draft or rejected
On rejection: status resets to draft (not rejected) — allows immediate edit and resubmit

CAMPAIGN STATUS MACHINE:
draft → submitted → (approved → active) | rejected
active → paused | closed | full
paused → active (resume)
full = max_applications reached (auto)
closed = end_date passed (auto) or manual
Editable when: draft or rejected

APPLICATION STATUS MACHINE:
applied → under_review | rejected | withdrawn
under_review → shortlisted | rejected
shortlisted → interview_scheduled | rejected
interview_scheduled → interviewed | rejected | withdrawn
interviewed → selected | waitlisted | rejected | withdrawn
selected → offer_pending | rejected | withdrawn
waitlisted → selected | rejected | withdrawn
offer_pending → offer_accepted | offer_declined
offer_accepted → hired
offer_declined → shortlisted (re-consideration)
hired → (terminal)
rejected → (terminal)
withdrawn → (terminal)
All transitions logged to recruitment_application_status_logs table.

INTERVIEW SCHEDULE STATUS MACHINE:
draft → submitted → (approved → scheduled) | rejected
scheduled → completed | cancelled
On approval: linked shortlisted applications auto-transition to interview_scheduled

OFFER STATUS MACHINE:
draft → submitted → (approved) | (rejected → draft)
approved → accepted | declined | expired | withdrawn
On approval: application→offer_pending, candidate emailed with portal link
On rejection: status resets to draft
expired = offer_expiry_date passed with no response (cron job)

APPROVAL WORKFLOW (applies to Plans, Campaigns, Interview Schedules, Offers):
- Three tables: workflows (per document_type), stages (ordered), records (per instance per cycle)
- submission_cycle increments on resubmission, historical records preserved
- Each stage has required_role (spatie role) and can_reject flag
- Records have snapshot columns: stage_order, stage_name, required_role
- Status values: Pending | Approved | Rejected

KEY BUSINESS RULES:
- One application per candidate per campaign (unique constraint)
- Department NEVER stored on campaigns — always derived via campaign→jobPosition→department
- Evaluation template auto-resolved from job_position_id at schedule creation (position-specific or NULL/"All" fallback)
- Offer salary should not exceed campaign salary_max
- Candidate code auto-generated: ID00001, ID00002 (with soft-deleted records counted)
- Campaign code auto-generated: CP_001, CP_002
- desired_salary and introduce_yourself are per-application fields (on applications table, NOT candidates)
- Candidates authenticate via portal guard (model extends Authenticatable, has password, portal_access, email_verified_at, remember_token)
- Evaluation overall_score computed: sum of (score × proportion/100) across all criteria
- Interview types: in_person | online | hybrid | telephone
- Interviewer roles: chair | interviewer | observer (observers cannot submit evaluations)
- Plans have working_from field: Internship | Full-Time | Part-Time | Contract | Temporary
- Plan salary range: salary_from, salary_to in salary_currency (ETB|USD|EUR)
- Channels have language support (en|am) and configurable notification targets
- Restrict on delete: job positions and departments cannot be deleted while plans/campaigns reference them
